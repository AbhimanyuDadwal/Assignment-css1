Avatar
>

The film is set in the mid-22nd century on Pandora, a moon in the Alpha Centauri star system. The protagonist, Jake Sully, a paraplegic Marine, becomes part of a mission to mine a valuable mineral, unobtanium. He uses an “avatar” identity to infiltrate the native Na’vi people, but ends up bonding with them and fighting against the human colonizers.
